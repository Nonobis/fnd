💡 Template Tips:
  • Use {{.Object}} for detected objects
  • Use {{.Camera}} for camera names
  • Use {{.Date}} and {{.Time}} for timestamps
  • Use {{.VideoURL}} for video links
  • Use {{if .HasVideo}}...{{end}} for conditional content
  • Use {{if .HasFaces}}...{{end}} for facial recognition content
  • Use {{.RecognizedFaces}} for recognized person names
  • Use {{.UnknownFaces}} for count of unknown faces
  • Use {{.FaceCount}} for total faces detected
⚠️ Important:

  • Empty templates fall back to global or default format
  • Test your templates before saving
