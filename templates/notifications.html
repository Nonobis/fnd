Help & Information

Cooldown Period

Prevents spam by setting a minimum time between notifications from the same camera. Recommended: 30-300 seconds.

Active Cameras

Only cameras marked as "Active" will send notifications. Inactive cameras will still record but won't trigger alerts.

💡 Tip: Start with a longer cooldown period and adjust based on your needs. Too short may result in notification spam.
⚠️ Note: Changes require the Frigate server to be properly configured and running.
